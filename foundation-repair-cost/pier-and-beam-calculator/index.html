The Core Elements of a Pier and Beam System

  • Footings distribute the vertical load into stable soil.
  • Piers support the main beams and keep the living floor elevated.
  • Sill plates and girders carry the structural frame.
  • Floor joists support the subfloor and finished flooring.

Professional Guidelines for Reviewing Contractor Bids

  • Request floor-elevation mapping before and after leveling.
  • Separate timber replacement pricing from general leveling labor.
  • Address drainage or moisture sources so the repair is not temporary.

Frequently Asked Questions

01 How do contractors level a pier and beam house?
They use hydraulic jacks to lift the frame gradually, then stabilize it with shims, repaired beams, or replacement piers depending on the cause.
02 How much does pier and beam foundation repair cost?
Many projects fall between $2,000 and $15,000, while severe rot or multiple failed piers can push higher.
03 What causes a crawl space foundation to sag?
Common causes include soft soil, poor drainage, high moisture, wood rot, undersized supports, or shifting piers.
